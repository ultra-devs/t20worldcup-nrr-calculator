How to use

Steps:

  • Select candidate and opponent teams (flags update automatically).
  • Choose whether candidate batted first.
  • Enter match innings: runs, overs and balls, wickets.
  • Optionally enter current tournament NRR to see an estimated updated NRR.
  • Click Calculate to show match NRR, estimated tournament NRR and the winner.

Note: Exact updated tournament NRR requires cumulative runs and overs; providing only NRR yields an estimate.

Enabled

Enter current tournament NRR if you don't want to provide cumulative totals.
